Frequently Asked Questions

How can I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions to reset it via email.
Where can I find my course schedule?
Your course schedule is available on your dashboard under "My Programs".
How long do I have access to course materials?
You will have access to all materials for 12 months from your course start date.
