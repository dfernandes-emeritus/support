Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find my program schedule?
You should be able to view the program schedule under Modules -> Program Oultine.
When can I apply for a leave of absence?
You should be able to view the program schedule under Modules -> Program Oultine.
Am I eligible for an extension?
Visit the EVA requirements page to check whether you are eligible to request for an extension. Please note EVA can only be raised not later than 3 days post an assignment due date.
How long do I have access to course materials?
You will have access to all materials for 12 months from your course end date.
What is the acceptable similarity index for assignments?
Anything between 0% to 5% falls in the scope of an acceptable similarity index.
Can I access Turnitin to check my writing?
The option to access Turnitin was discussed by Professor Phil in the webinar. More information will be provided soon.
Is in-text citation needed for every table, graph, or paragraph?
Yes, proper citation is expected wherever external data or references are used, including tables and graphs.
Do I need to reference statistical data or UWA case studies even if I paraphrase them?
Yes, you must provide a reference even if you paraphrase statistical data or UWA case study content. Paraphrasing without referencing may still be considered plagiarism.
Are AI-generated images allowed in assignments?
Use of AI-generated images is not encouraged. If used, they must be clearly acknowledged and comply with academic integrity guidelines.
What happens if I receive an N-Fail in a module?
If you receive an N-Fail, you may have to repeat the unit in the next teaching period. Repeated N-Fails could result in suspension for one teaching period.
